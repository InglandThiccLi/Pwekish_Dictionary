About this website

Welcome to the Pwekish Online Dictionary. Here, you access the lastest and most complete resources of the Pwekish language. Key features include word searching, language learning and e-books viewing. We aim to provide a user-friendly platform for the public to learn and appreciate the beauty of language, so as to ignite their interest in learning a foreign language, digging deep into the world of linguistic, or even create their own facsinating language from scratch!

What is Pwekish?

Pwekish is a constructed language created by CheckedMorrow and InglandThiccLi. It belongs to the Pwekian language family, which has no clear relationship with other major language families of the world. It also exhibits a special grammatical structure and exotic set of root words. For instance, Pwekish is an agglutinative language, which allows the stacking of word elements for the formation of long words, enabling many Pwekish-originated words to be derived from a small set of basic root words. Besides, Pwekish has total 4 different writing system, which are “BentolRomak”, “Punloy BentolPwek”, “Kentan BentolPwek”, and “Handzi”. The later 3 are often used together in a text while “BentolRomak” is often used alone. All these makes Pwekish a distinct language.
