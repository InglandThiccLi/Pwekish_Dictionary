Chapter 1: The Basics

Introduction

Nyebo! (Hello!) Nice to see you in the first lesson! This foundational chapter will introduce you to the essential elements of Pwekish that every learner needs to master. We'll start with the alphabet system called bentolRomak, explore the letters and their pronunciation, and then move on to building your first sentences using common vocabulary. By the end of this chapter, you'll also be introduced to one of Pwekish's most distinctive grammatical features - Personal Verb Initial Mutation. These fundamentals will serve as your stepping stones to fluency in this unique language.
