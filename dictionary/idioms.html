Pwekish Idioms

Idioms

Pwekish idioms are figurative phrases or short sentence which often backed with a story or requires some level of understanding to the Pwekish culture. Most of the time, these expressions are linguistically intricate and deeply evocative.

Idioms are often used in creative writing, though some of them are used frequently in daily conversation. In writing, they are traditionally rendered in "Handzi" form with some "BentolPwek". Yet, for simplicity and accessibility, using "BentolRomak" is also accepted nowadays.

To sum up, Pwekish idioms are highly cultural expressions which showcase not only the creativity of the language, but also the versatility of the language's structure and script.
