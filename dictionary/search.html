Tutorial

General Tips
  1. Enter the Pwekish term or English meaning (only for advanced search).
  2. Click the search button or press "ENTER" to search.
  3. After loading, you will see the result in the box below.
  4. Click the word entry to view the details.
  5. If you cannot perform search, please ensure that your input contains only:
    • English characters (A-Z and a-z)
    • "SPACE"
    • "á","é","í","ó","ú","Á","É","Í","Ó","Ú"
    • "Ñ","ñ","Ņ","ņ","`","'"
  6. To facilitate searching of verbs, please enter the to-infinitive form (e.g. to eat).
Pagination
  1. You can adjust the number of entries shown by setting the "show number" at the bottom-left of the results.
  2. You can turn to different page by clicking the buttons at the bottom-right of the results.
Simple Search
  1. Enabled when the "Advanced Search" checkbox is not checked.
  2. Simple search does not allow you to search by English meaning.
  3. Plus, the search only gives you words which start with the first letter of your input.
  4. This search mode is faster.
Advanced Search
  1. Enabled when the "Advanced Search" checkbox is checked.
  2. Advanced search allows you to search by English meaning.
  3. It also allows you to find any words that match your input.
  4. This search mode is slower.
